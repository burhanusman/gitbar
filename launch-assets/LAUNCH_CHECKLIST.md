**Question: "How is this different from terminal?"**
```
Great question!

GitBar is about awareness without context switching. You could check terminal every time, but GitBar gives you that info at a glance - like checking your watch vs pulling out your phone.

It's for when you want to know "do I have uncommitted work?" without breaking flow.
```

**Question: "Does it slow down my Mac?"**

## Red Flags / Warning Signs

### If Engagement is Low
- Don't panic on day 1 - quality > quantity
- Try different content types
- Ask community what they want to see
- Engage more authentically
- Check if technical issues are preventing access

### If Negative Feedback
- Don't take it personally
- Listen and thank them
- Fix valid issues quickly
- Explain reasoning for design decisions
- Turn critics into contributors

### If Technical Issues
- Address immediately - launch can wait
- Be transparent about problems
- Post status updates
- Offer workarounds if possible
- Follow up when fixed

### What NOT to Do
- ‚ùå Buy upvotes/followers/engagement
- ‚ùå Spam tag influencers
- ‚ùå Delete criticism
- ‚ùå Argue with users
- ‚ùå Over-promise features
- ‚ùå Make excuses for bugs
- ‚ùå Ignore feedback
- ‚ùå Forget to eat/sleep (seriously, take care of yourself!)
