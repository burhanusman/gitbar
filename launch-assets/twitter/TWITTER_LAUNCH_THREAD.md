## Crisis Management

### If Things Go Wrong

**Bug Reports**
- Acknowledge immediately
- Don't make excuses
- Provide timeline for fix
- Follow up when resolved

**Negative Feedback**
- Listen and thank them
- Ask clarifying questions
- Fix if valid, explain if not
- Turn critics into contributors

**Low Engagement**
- Don't panic on day 1
- Quality > quantity
- Engage authentically
- Try different content types
- Ask community what they want

### Red Flags to Avoid
- âŒ Buying followers/engagement
- âŒ Spam tagging influencers
- âŒ Deleting criticism
